How to find your Device Code

Open Settings in the app.
  1. Open Caffeine Tracker on your watch.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Select Enter premium code.
  4. On the next screen, copy your Device Code.
  5. Click Buy unlock and paste the Device Code into the Stripe checkout.
Important: The Device Code is unique to your watch.
Please copy it exactly (no extra spaces). If you enter a wrong Device Code, contact support with your receipt.
